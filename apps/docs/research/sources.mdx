## Method

<Info>
Run 2026‑06‑20. **6 search angles** → **29 sources fetched** → **135 claims extracted** →
**25 adversarially verified** (3-vote; a claim is killed only on 2/3 refute) → **24 confirmed, 1
killed** → synthesized to 11 findings. Confidence labels (`high` / `medium`) reflect source quality
and corroboration, and are preserved on each research page.
</Info>

The six angles: underlying-asset mechanics · compute-commodity analogues · perp DEX & oracle design ·
derivatives & oracle market design · regulatory/legal exposure · contrarian feasibility (does
volatility & hedging demand exist).

## Source-quality caveats

<Warning>
- **arXiv:2603.21690** is a **single-author, non-peer-reviewed preprint**; its 62–78% / 87% hedging
  numbers are outputs of the author's *own simulation of a non-existent market*. Use the SIT *idea*,
  not the figures.
- **CME+Silicon Data and ICE+Ornn** were announced **~1 month before** the research and are **pending
  regulatory review — not live**. They validate feasibility, not success.
- **Silicon Data** is a **single-vendor proprietary** index.
- Every strong real-world precedent tracks **GPU compute**, not **LLM tokens** — structural
  analogues, not direct precedents.
- [SF Compute's market docs](https://docs.sfcompute.com/current/how-the-market-works) were rated
  **unreliable** in this pass (no verified claims extracted).
</Warning>
